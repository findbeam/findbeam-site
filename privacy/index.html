Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 17, 2025

FindBeam ("we," "our," or "us") operates as an independent web service. We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights under global regulations, including the GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA (California), and LGPD (Brazil).

1. Data Collection & Usage

We adhere to the principle of Data Minimization. We only collect data necessary to provide our services, secure our infrastructure, and maintain our business operations.

A. Information You Provide

  • Tool Input Data: When you use tools like our JSON Formatter or Minifiers, the code you input is processed locally in your browser (Client-Side) whenever technically feasible. We do not store your code snippets on our servers.
  • Communication: If you contact us at legal@findbeam.com, we collect your email address and message content solely to respond to your inquiry.

B. Automated Technologies (Cookies & Pixels)

We use third-party tracking technologies to analyze traffic, secure the site, and serve advertisements. We manage your consent preferences using Silktide.

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function (e.g., security checks via Cloudflare Turnstile).
  • Analytics (Google Analytics 4): Helps us understand which tools are most popular. IP addresses are anonymized where possible.
  • Marketing (Meta Pixel): Used to measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns.

2. Advertising & Affiliate Disclosure

To keep FindBeam free, we monetize our traffic through the following methods:

A. Display Advertising (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to display ads. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

B. Affiliate Links

FindBeam participates in affiliate marketing programs. If you click on a link to a recommended product (e.g., hosting services or developer tools) and make a purchase, we may receive a commission at no additional cost to you. This does not influence our editorial integrity.

3. International Data Transfers (US Hosting)

FindBeam is hosted on servers located in the United States. If you are accessing our site from the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, Brazil, or Asia:

  • Your personal data (e.g., IP address, browser strings) will be transferred to and processed in the United States.
  • The United States may not have data protection laws equivalent to those in your jurisdiction.
  • By using our services, you acknowledge and consent to this transfer. We rely on your consent and the necessity of performance of a contract (providing the tool you requested) for these transfers.

4. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have specific rights regarding your data:

  • GDPR (EU/UK) & LGPD (Brazil): Right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your data. You also have the right to withdraw consent for cookies via the Silktide banner at any time.
  • CCPA/CPRA (California): You have the right to know what personal info is collected and the right to delete it. We do not sell your personal data to data brokers.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at legal@findbeam.com.

5. Consent Management

We use Silktide to manage cookie consent compliance. When you first visit our site, you are presented with a banner allowing you to accept or reject non-essential cookies. You can update your preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies for this domain.
